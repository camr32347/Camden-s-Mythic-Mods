Better Splashes

Atmospheric splash texts for the Minecraft title screen

Better Splashes is a minimalist Minecraft resource pack that replaces the default splash texts with cryptic, mythic, and surreal alternatives. No textures. No sounds. No gameplay tweaks. Just words — drifting across the void with quiet intent.

  • Hand-curated splashes.txt with custom flavor
  • Clean and lightweight — no panoramas or assets
  • Pack metadata and icon included
  • Compatible with Minecraft 1.20.1+
